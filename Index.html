About BSIS

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) is a 4-year program blending business and IT. It trains students to solve business problems using technology, preparing them for careers in systems analysis, IT management, and digital transformation.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Information Systems Analysis and Design
  • Web and Mobile App Development
  • Database Management
  • Business Process Management
  • Enterprise Systems
  • IT Project Management
  • Capstone Project and Internship

Tuition Fees

Average tuition ranges from ₱20,000 to ₱40,000 per semester depending on the school. State universities may offer free tuition under CHED or government scholarships.

Facilities

  • High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • Digital Library Access
  • Innovation and IT Hubs
  • Industry-Experienced Faculty
